Science And Technology Entrepreneurs Park (STEP)

Science & Technology Entrepreneurs Park (STEP) was established in the year 2001

Activities:

  • It contains executive taining programmes for the industrial enterprises & growth oriented programmes for the first & second generation entrepreneurs.
  • It provides sopport service to entrepreneurs, escorts services to the first generatin entrepreneurs.
  • It conducts various seminars & workshops in the related fields of interest & need.
  • It develops strong database on all the industrial activities, govt. policies etc.
  • It posses a strong network with the FI & supporting agencies.
  • It promotes entrepreneurs in the thrust areas.
  • It conducts techno-economic feasibility services.
  • It develops products & processes.
  • It provides infrastructure facilities & technical support for incubation of the opportunities.

Programmes Conducted:

  • E-Commerce.
  • Computer Assembly & Service.
  • Office Automation & Tally.
  • Beauty Parlour.
  • Bakery Products.
  • PMRY-EDP.
  • EAC(In house & other)
