  Research & Development Center

         The Department of Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering & Department of Chemistry are recognized as research centers by the V.T.U, Belgaum. About 30 faculty members are persuing for their doctoral program.

         To update the knowledge of faculty members, invited lectures & workshops are conducted regularly. Central facilities like Geographical Information Systems & Microprocessor labs are being established.

The area of interests are:

  • Computers
  • Mathematics
  • Electronics
  • Mechanical
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Chemistry
  • Civil Engineering
