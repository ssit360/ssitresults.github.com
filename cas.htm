Center For Advanced Studies (CAS)

Center For Advanced Studies (CAS) was established in the year 2002.

Objectives:

  • To turn out world class professionals in the field of Engineering & technology.
  • To provide professionals skill orinted industry based higher-end training in a joint venture with world class industries.
  • To make the professionals ready to be observed by leading co-orperate across the globe.
  • To equip with best lab & facilities to bridge the gap between the academics & industry.
It provides training in the field of:
  • Embedded Systems - Design & Development.
  • Wireless Technology & Mobile Communication.
  • Digital Signaling Processing.
  • Networkingm, VLSI & SOC.
  • J2E & DOT NET
  • AUTO CAD, M-CAD etc.,
  • Open Learning Diploma in Entrepreneurship & Bussiness Management.
Uniqueness of Programs is:
  • Industry based Curriculum.
  • Seminars & Group Discussions.
  • Guest Lecture from Industry Experts.
  • Live Projects (Both Minor & Major).
  • Training on Soft Skills.
  • Industry Visits & placement Assistance.
This Programme is not a course but a professional training; G-MEDIC will jointly with UTL & EduCADD certify the successful candidates as "PROFESSIONALS".

Various Programs Conducted by:
  • Professional Training to Engineers on "EMBEDDED SYSTEMS".
  • Professional Training on "C-PROGRAMMING".
  • Professional Training on :"Pro-E".
  • Professional Training on Auto-CADD 2005.
