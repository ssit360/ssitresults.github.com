CULTURAL ACTIVITIES

         SSIT has taken keen interest to explore the talents of its students through annual cultural festival "KALOTSAVA". The fest is scooped with different events to showcase & hone cultural, technical & literary skills of the students. The students from various technical institutions in the Karnataka state participate in the KALOTSAVA festival which includes varieties of events & are aimed at increasing the friendship & exchange of ideas among the students of different institutions.

         Our college has gained reputation by winning awards & trophies in the fests of the other technical institution & universities.
