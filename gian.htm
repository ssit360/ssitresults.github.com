Grassroots Innovation & Augmentation Network Cell (GIAN-Cell)

         The Grassroots Innovation & Augmentation Network Cell (GIAN-CELL) was established in the year 2004. It is a unique centre for Documentation, Evaluation & incubation of Grassroots Innovation South India & particularly in Karnataka established in the association with "The National Innovation Foundation (NIF)", an organ of Department of Science & Technology, GOI.

         The Aim of this cell is to act as a nodal centre to upgrade, design & development & commercialize the Grassroots innovation in Karnataka.

Objectives:

  • Rural Industrial development through incubation of local innovation.
  • IPR Management & providing local R & D support.
  • Value addition to local innovation using existing facilities at Technical Institutions.
  • Setting up local incubation centre through participation of students.

Activities:

  • Documentation & evaluation of grassroots innovation scouted bu NIF.
  • Assists the innovation for up-gradation of thier innovations.
  • Process development for commercialization of the innovations.
  • RE-design & development of the innovations through student projects (Technology Incubation)
  • Carrying out business plan for the innovations
  • Assisting for the innovations
  • Incubating of the feasible and viable innovations (Technology Business Incubation)

Achivements:

  • Two Projects were developed by GIAN-CELL.
  • 15 Projects on hand.
  • Two Projects got National Recognition.
  • 10 Projects were documented.
