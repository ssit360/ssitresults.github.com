Gangadharaiah-Memorial Entrepreneurship Development & Incubation Centre (G-MEDIC)

Vision:

We, at G-medic impart skills, both hard & soft, to our students & youth to become more Entrepreneurial to face the global standards through dedicated training, who inturn will improve the quality of life of the human race, hence make "OUR INDIA" innovative.
 

Mission:

Our mission is to educate Science and Technology youth from all walks of life from rural and urban areas to become euphoric individuals by practicing Enterpreneurship , and improve their ability in identification of opportunities through self appraisal of SWOT analysis.

Lt. L Sanjeev kumar
Director, G-MEDIC

         Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology has taken a leading role to uphold the entrepreneurial culture in Tumkur District. To motivate & guide young entrepreneurs by giving consultancy & supporting services. Department of Industries & Commerce, Govt. of Karnataka has sanctioned Science & Technology Entrepreneurs Park (STEP) to the college in the year 2000 & it has been recognised as one of the best training center in the district by various Govt. agencies & Industries. It is named in the remembrance of our beloved founder Sri. H.M Gangadharaiah, as a Gangadharaiah - Memorial Entrepreneurship Development & Incubation Center (G-MEDIC). Under this scheme SSIT is conducting various skills & entrepreneurship Development programmes.
