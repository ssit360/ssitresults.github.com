GEO-DESIC DOME Central & Digital Library (Sixth in world)

Vision:

To develop as an excellent centre for information comprising of print and digital media of information and create a platform to the users to acquire the required information under a single root.
 

Mission:

To provide the users with an opportunity to enhance their knowledge with an independent opening for learning beyond the curriculum.


         The Library is housed in a unique Geodesic Dome that is aesthetically structured to act as an Energy Center providing inspiration for the students to get into reading habit. It has 4 floors on the reading side with a seating capacity of 500 and 5 floors on the stocking side to stock 1,00,000 books. At present the Library has 67,000 volumes and subscription to 156 National and International journals. It has a seperate fully air conditioned , Digital Library with a seating capacity of 40. The Institute is member of DELNET, and JCCC.

        The centre also has of collection of corporate annual reports pertaining to private / public sector undertakings and project reports on different areas of research. The library is laid out spaciously. Its eco-friendly environment provides calm and peaceful environment for learning.
