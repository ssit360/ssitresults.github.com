IT-Incubation Center

Activities:

  • It assists the youth in preparing a project report & carry out the feasibility study.
  • It provides the assistance in procurement of systems & office euipments.
  • It provides internet accessibility, if required.
  • It provides clerical assistance.
  • It provides assistance on job work & office automation.
  • It provides guidance in establishing network with the bankers & other service providers.
  • It assists the incubates in supervising their day-to-day activities.
  • It provides assistance to scan the marketing environments.
  • It assists the incubates in expansion of their venture through internal & external sources.
  • It undertakes the civil & technical projects of the incubates when they want to establish their own infrastructure before they leave incubation center.
  • It gives follow-up support for few months when the incubates established their own independent venture, after they left the incubation center.
