Our Principal



Dr.K.A.Krishna Murthy
B.E, M.Tech, Ph.D, PEngg(Canada), MA.Sc(USA), SM(IEEE), FIETE, FIE
PRINCIPAL

Recieved the Best Principal National Award for the year 2010 from ISTE

           Dr. K A Krishna Murthy, popularly known as K.A.K, is an internationally renowned engineering teacher, researcher, writer and educationist. Before taking over as the principal of Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology (SSIT), he also has served as the Principal of Malnad Engineering College, Hassan, from 1997.

         He holds Ph.D from IIT, Mumbai. Besides teaching, which is his passion, Dr. Murthy has also tried his hand in research. He was a Research Fellow at the University of Manitoba in Canada during 1981-82. Later in his career he was a Visiting Professor at the same university. He was a Visiting Consulting Professor at the Asea Brown Boveri, Switzerland during 1988-89.

         In an unblemished teaching career spanning over three and half a decades, Dr. Krishna Murthy has won several laurels and published many books, and presented numerous papers at many international conferences. He was a recipient of several awards and won accolades over the years.
